Our Story

Since 2007, Kalesa Restaurant has been a cherished part of the Bay Area's Filipino community. We are a family-owned establishment, born from a passion for sharing the rich, traditional flavors of our homeland.

Our family moved from the Philippines in 2001, bringing with us generations of experience in the restaurant business. We opened Kalesa to create a space that feels like home, serving authentic dishes crafted from family recipes.

From classic adobo and kare-kare to festive platters, our mission is to serve timeless Filipino cuisine. We also offer full-service catering to bring the taste of Kalesa to your special events.
